Making Simple Task Easier

Emerkado is a mobile-based application which is designed for a new normal adaptation to remotely buy and sell goods. With this, people can sell, or order goods without hesitation given this pandemic. The project aims to provide convenience to both parties as it will help those who have anxiety to go to the market, or those who does not have time and energy to prepare ingredients for their food. To the sellers on the other hand, this will help them increase their profit as their will be customers online aside from the customers who will visit their market stalls.
