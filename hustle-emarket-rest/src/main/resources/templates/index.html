Making Simple Task Easier

Emerkado is a mobile-based application, made to revolutionalize the way of acquiring/selling grocery.

Doing things remotely has become popular to most businesses, and soon, will be the norm. But not all can create their respective online shops, especially those who are selling in the markets, with their perishable items.

Emerkado aims to provide convenience to both parties.

To the customers: it will help those who have anxiety to go to the market, or those who does not have time and energy to spare, visiting the physical market building, to acquire grocery items without sacrificing it's quality over comfort.

To the sellers: This will be their opportunity to advertise their items online and increase their profit, as there will be customers online aside from the customers who will visit their market stalls. The possibility of a store becoming famous for their items will also be a big factor, for satisfied customers will surely suggest the store to other potential customers.
